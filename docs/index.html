How to use
1
Confirm the lab Mac is on and ./start.sh is running in Terminal
2
Tap Launch Lab App above — the app opens in a new tab If it doesn't load, you may not be on lab WiFi
3
Scan or enter the participant's SONA ID to print their label
4
Collect the label from the participant at the end of the session
Camera scanning works on the iPad and lab Mac only.
On a phone, use Manual Entry or the USB scanner at the desktop.
